Discover Europe

travel across Europe with this excellent travel agency

Discover Europe is a travel agency that provides a help with one's itinerary including flights, lodging, and attractions. Discover Europe asked for a ten page responsive website design, which was built with Bootstrap 5 in Adobe Dreamweaver. They also specifically asked for a built in Contact Form which can be found on their Contact page. Discover Europe's website includes photos of each city included in the nine countries they provide trips to. Thanks to their new, helpful contact form, they also offer student interships. Adobe Photoshop was used for some of the images.
